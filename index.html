ABOUT ME

Let me Introduce myself

Hello again, I'm Michael Bermal a Web Developer from Bocaue, Bulacan and I love building things. I was a Civil Engineering working student way back in college but I wasn't able to finish it with one semester left since I was tasked to takeover my family's business. Honestly this is a new thing for me but I'm so interested in computer stuffs since I was just a kid. And I think if I would like to change my career path then why not try something I am interested in.

What I used to do?

After College I was doing a lot of stuff in our small construction business. At first I was just a Document Controller when I was a student, but later on I became a Site Engineer, a Quantity Surveyor and sometimes Project-in-charge. Before I decided to change career i was a Contractor. Most of our project deals with Steel Structures on hospitals, gymnasiums and warehouses. but during the pandemic I think that career was not for me mentally and financially. And since I want to become a better version of myself I tried coding as a new skill and career to pursue.

Why work with me?

I am analytical, I take complex problems and turn it into something simple. I am also eager to finish any project earlier than its deadline as much as possible. I can do Front End Web Development. In my free time I play video games, try my culinary skill from time to time or play basketball with my friends.
